About

I am a Lecturer in the School of Mathematical Sciences at South China Normal University, since October 2025.

I received my PhD from Beijing Normal University in June 2025 under the supervision of Professor Jianquan Ge. My research interests is differential geometry.

My work lies at the rigidity of minimal submanifolds in space forms, with particular interests in topological and geometric properties of submanifolds.

Research

Broadly speaking, I am interested in the rigidity of minimal surfaces immersed in space forms, especially in unit spheres. My recent work focuses on Udo Simon's conjecture, Lu's conjecture and some other rigidity problems.
